About Me

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in RAM Lab, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(HKUST), supervised by Ming Liu. I received the B.S.(2012) and the M.S.(2014) in Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT). In the last year of the undergraduate, I joined the ABU Robocon as a team member of HIT Competitive Robot Team. Since then, I started my robotics work. In 2017, I was a visiting researcher in AIS, University of Freiburg, working with Joschka Boedecker and Wolfram Burgard. I was also with City University of Hong Kong from Sep 2015 to Aug 2017 which is another long story...
